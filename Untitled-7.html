Mehendi is an auspicious pre-wedding ritual for any Indian bride. India is a vast country with varied customs and traditions but the ritual of Mehendi is celebrated pretty much the same in all the cultures and religions. Beautiful Mehendi designs symbolizing love are put on the brides hands by professional henna artists. KRISHNA EVENTS understands the importance of traditional ceremonies, so we maintain the true essence of the tradition and add a modern twist to it. Mehendi is defined by singing, dancing, food and fun. KRISHNA EVENTS aims to make your Mehendi a spectacular event handling every aspect of it.

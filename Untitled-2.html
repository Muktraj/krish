One of the most significant and important day in your lives is your Wedding day. Communication is an important aspect for its success. More often than not it is ignored by most people, leaving this important element of the wedding to chance. KRISHNA works with you to come up with stylish and innovative way to reach out to your friends and family. Wedding communication involves elements like Invitations, Stationary, Thank You Cards, Save The Date Cards and Wedsites & WedApps. A Wedsite is a digital diary of your wedding details, it is also the easiest way to organize your wedding and keep your guests informed. A WedApp is Mobile Friendly Diary of your Wedding Details keeping your Guests informed and Notified about your Wedding Details.
